30-second elevator pitch

"I'm an operations strategist with a Deloitte consulting foundation and 2 years at Adler Pelzer, a global automotive Tier-1. I run cross-plant strategy across 16 sites, lead CAPEX feasibility (NPV / IRR / payback) and present insourcing and footprint cases to CEO and COO level. I'm now looking to bring that strategic-economic toolkit to a more travel-heavy, international role."

Case framework (6 steps)
  1. CLARIFY the question & scope
  2. STRUCTURE with a MECE tree
  3. ANALYZE the main drivers
  4. QUANTIFY the numbers
  5. RECOMMEND clearly
  6. RISK & next steps
Top 5 metrics to always mention
  1. NPV (5-yr horizon)
  2. IRR vs hurdle rate
  3. Simple payback (years)
  4. EBITDA impact (€ / %)
  5. ROCE / ROI
